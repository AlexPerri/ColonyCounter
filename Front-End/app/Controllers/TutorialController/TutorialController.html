ColonyCounter is a web app that is used to count the number of
yeast colonies in an image of a petri dish.

To upload an image to analyze:

1. Click the Side Panel (the 3 bars) in the top left.

2. Click "Process Images".

3. Click "Choose file" and select the image(s) you wish to upload.
From here, you can change the files you have chosen or click "Submit".

*You can navigate to your image history via the Side Panel in the top left
